### 🧠 About Me

I architect systems that learn, adapt, and reason. 

I live at the intersection of **rigorous research** and **creative application**. Whether I'm deconstructing the latest transformer architecture or optimizing a production pipeline, my goal remains the same: to make the complex feel simple. I don't just write code; I design experiences that bridge the gap between human intuition and machine precision.

- 🔭 Currently exploring: **LLM Context Compression**

---

### 🧩 The Philosophy

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." — *Leonardo da Vinci*

In the world of high-dimensional matrices and loss functions, I believe the most elegant solution is often the simplest. I approach every problem with a researcher's curiosity and a designer's eye, ensuring that the systems I build are not only powerful but also intuitive.
